The following is a list of edge cases to think about when implementing time and date.


* Leap seconds
* Leap years
* Daylight savings time
* Time zones
* Time zones that change over time
* Time zones that change over time and have daylight savings time
* Time zones that change over time and have daylight savings time and have leap seconds
* Time zones that change over time and have daylight savings time and have leap seconds and have leap years
* Time zones that change over time and have daylight savings time and have leap seconds and have leap years and have leap seconds in leap years
* Different formats for time and date
